Copyright, What's That?

Copyright is a vital piece of data used to protect and claim ownership of an intellectual property. There are many forms of copyrights all with different effects, costs, and levels of privacy. Depending on what license one may choose for a work can change the way that the work can be distributed/replicated/monetised to varying degrees.
